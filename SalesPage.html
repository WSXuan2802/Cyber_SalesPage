Key features

Health Monitoring and Emergency Features

The Series 10 includes advanced health features such as ECG, blood oxygen monitoring, fall detection, and emergency SOS. These functionalities help track your overall health and provide critical assistance in case of an emergency.

Fitness and Activity Tracking

The Series 10 tracks a wide range of physical activities, from walking and running to swimming and cycling, while monitoring vital metrics like calories burned, heart rate, and workout duration.

Smart Notifications and Communication

The Apple Watch Series 10 allows you to receive and respond to notifications, calls, messages, and emails directly from your wrist, keeping you connected even when your phone isn't within reach.
